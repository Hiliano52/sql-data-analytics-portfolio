# 📖 Enterprise Customer Success & Churn Mitigation Playbook

A structured framework designed to manage multi-million-dollar B2B account lifecycles, minimize Time-to-Value (TTV), and maintain Net Revenue Retention (NRR > 105%).

---

## 🎯 1. Account Health Score Matrix

| Health Grade | Criteria / Metrics | Action Required |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Green (Healthy)** | NRR > 110%, high feature adoption, < 2 monthly escalation tickets. | Quarterly Business Review (QBR) & expansion push. |
| **Yellow (At-Risk)** | Drop in login activity, open SLA tickets > 5 days, TTV delay. | Trigger CS intervention & SQL usage audit. |
| **Red (Critical)** | Active churn risk, open billing/technical blockers, executive escalation. | Deploy Executive Churn Protocol within 24h. |

---

## 🚀 2. 30-60-90 Day Onboarding Framework

* **Days 1–30 (Implementation & TTV):** Complete CRM migration, train core users, verify database integrations. Target: Reduce Time-to-Value by 25%.
* **Days 31–60 (Adoption & SLA Audit):** Monitor ticket trends, optimize support triage, conduct mid-onboarding review.
* **Days 61–90 (Value Realization):** Establish baseline health score, align expansion targets, transition to standard CS lifecycle.
